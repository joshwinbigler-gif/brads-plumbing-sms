Privacy Policy – Brad's Plumbing Field Support SMS

Effective Date: June 25, 2026
Business: Brad's Plumbing
Contact: joshwinbigler@gmail.com

1. Overview

Brad's Plumbing operates an SMS-based field support service that allows plumbing technicians to send questions, descriptions, and photos of plumbing fixtures and receive automated diagnostic guidance via text message. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information transmitted through this service.

2. Who Uses This Service

This service is available exclusively to employees and authorized technicians of Brad's Plumbing. It is not a public-facing consumer service. No third-party solicitation or marketing is conducted through this number.

3. Information We Collect

When a technician sends a text message to our support number, we may collect:

  • The technician's mobile phone number
  • The content of the text message (including any description of the plumbing issue)
  • Any photos or media attached to the message
  • Date and time of the message
  • The automated response generated by our system

We do not collect sensitive personal information such as Social Security numbers, financial account information, or health data through this service.

4. How We Use This Information

Information collected through this SMS service is used solely to:

  • Generate and deliver a diagnostic or troubleshooting response to the technician
  • Log interactions for quality improvement and training purposes
  • Build internal reference data to improve the accuracy of future responses

We do not sell, share, or rent your phone number or message content to any third party for marketing purposes.

5. Message Frequency

Technicians receive one automated reply per message sent. Message frequency varies based on how often the technician contacts the support line. Standard message and data rates from your mobile carrier may apply.

6. Opt-Out

At any time, a technician may reply STOP to unsubscribe from receiving further automated responses. After opting out, no further messages will be sent unless the technician re-initiates contact. To re-subscribe, reply START.

7. Help

For assistance, reply HELP to the support number or contact us at joshwinbigler@gmail.com.

8. Data Retention

Message logs are retained for up to 12 months for internal quality review purposes and are then deleted. Data is stored securely and access is restricted to authorized personnel of Brad's Plumbing.

9. Third-Party Services

This service uses Twilio, Inc. to transmit SMS messages. Message content may pass through Twilio's infrastructure in accordance with Twilio's Privacy Policy. Automated responses are generated using Claude, an AI service by Anthropic, PBC, in accordance with Anthropic's Privacy Policy.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the Effective Date above. Continued use of the SMS service following any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
